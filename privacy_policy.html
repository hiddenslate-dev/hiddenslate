Privacy Policy

Here is the privacy policy for the Hiddenslate Basic API.

Whom is the Data Collected From

Hiddenslate will collect data posted by its customer companies and comments posted on the profiles of the aforementioned companies. Data collected about the customer companies will include:

  • media capton text (caption)
  • media id (id)
  • media type (media_type)
  • media url (media_url)
  • media publish date (timestamp)
  • media owner's username (username)

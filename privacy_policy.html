Privacy Policy

Here is the privacy policy for the Hiddenslate Basic API.

Whom is the Data Collected From

Hiddenslate will collect data posted by its customer companies and comments posted on the profiles of the aforementioned companies.

Data Hiddenslate Will Collect

Data collected about the customer companies will include:

  • media capton text (caption)
  • media id (id)
  • media type (media_type)
  • media url (media_url)
  • media publish date (timestamp)
  • media owner's username (username)

Data collected by public accounts that don't belong to customer companies will include:

  • comments made on media posted by customer companies

What Hiddenslate Will Do With the Data

Hiddenslate will not make public any of the data collected. All of the collected data will be used to perform sentiment analysis. The sentiment analysis will be performed on caption text of the media posted by the customer companies and on comments posted by other users on the media posted by these customer companies.
